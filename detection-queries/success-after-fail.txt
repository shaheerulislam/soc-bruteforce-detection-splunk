SOC Detection Rule: Successful Login After Multiple Failures

Threat Type:
Possible account compromise after brute-force attempt

Detection Logic:
Detects successful login following multiple failed attempts from same IP.

Splunk Query:
index=auth_logs sourcetype=linux_secure
("Failed password" OR "Accepted password")
| stats count by src_ip, user, message

Purpose:
Identifies successful authentication after brute-force attempts.
